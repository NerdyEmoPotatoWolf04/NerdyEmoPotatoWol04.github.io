Why I am inspired by her

Millie Bobby Brown is a very unique individual, she is an English Actor and Model. Her very first big hit was in 2016 in "Stranger Things" where she played the role of "Eleven". She has earned two Nominee OFTA Television Award for being "Best Supporting Actress in a Drama Series Stranger Things (2016)" she earned those in the years of 2017-2018.

Timeline of Millie's life

  • Millie was born 19 February 2004
  • In 2013, Brown made her acting debut as a guest star in the ABC fantasy drama series Once Upon a Time in Wonderland, a spin-off of Once Upon a Time, portraying the role of Young Alice. In 2014
  • In November 2018, Millie became UNICEF's youngest Goodwill Ambassador, pledging to speak out for the millions of children whose voices are silenced across the world, empower them and make sure they know their rights. Millie is also actively involved in UNICEF's #GoBlue campaign, for World Children's Day.
  • So far, Millie has won nine awards including one SAG Award for 'Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series' which she shares with her Stranger Things cast mates
  • Brown made her feature film debut in 2019, in the sequel to Godzilla, titled Godzilla: King of the Monsters. She will reprise her role in Godzilla vs. Kong.
  • Millie was born in Marbella, Andalusia, Spain
